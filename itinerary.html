Itinerary

It is a three-week program which runs from July 22nd to August 12th. Unlike normal Chinese summer camp, our study tour highlights the topic of Chinese cuisine, and integrates intensive Chinese language and culture courses with extracurricular activities to make sure that language learning and cultural exchange can be put into practice by students. All the Chinese culture courses are designed by our certified mandarin teachers. Our students will learn about Chinese language and culture in the morning, and then combine their knowledge with first-hand experiences in the following activities.

*Please note that our schedule is subject to minor changes on account of bad weather and other uncontrollable factors.
 *Local time and dates in Beijing. (GMT+8 Time Zones)
