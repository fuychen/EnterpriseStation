Application & Enrollment Procedure
Please note that students intend to participate in 2017 PZ Interculture summer Study Tour should finish the enrollment procedure on or before enrollment deadline (June 30th). The instructions are as follows.
Step 1—application
1
For individual participants, download the attached application materials, fill out the forms in detail and pay the deposit, USD $500/person**. Scan and send the filled application materials and deposit remittance note to apply@pzinterculture.com by email, then you will receive a confirmation letter within 3 business days.
For group members, finish the individual application materials first, and then you also need to read and then fill out the Contact Person Form together, so that each member in a group will get the group discount respectively.
Application Form
Medical Treatment Authorization
Release and Waiver of Liability
Non-negotiable Study Tour Rules
Contact Person Form (for group applications only)
Step 2—registration
2
Finish the rest of your payment, or your group’s payment before Jun. 30**. Scan the remittance note that your bank issued and email it to apply@pzinterculture.com. We will issue the Registration Verification and email it to you together with the packing list within 3 business days once we received your payment.
*Please note the deadline for early bird ticket is May 31.
**All the payment should be paid by one of the following methods:
1. Wire Transfer/Bank Transfer
2. Paypal
Step 3—Preparation
3
Apply for your passport and visa. Book flight tickets and let us know about your flight information. Remember to print out your Registration Verification and take it with you to Beijing.
